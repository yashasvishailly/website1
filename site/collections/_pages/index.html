Mobile Disinfection Unit

Our Mobile Disinfection Unit (MDU) provides the ultimate cleaning and disinfection for patient care equipment. Avoid wasting time with hand scrubbing or using harsh power washing that can damage equipment. We can quickly and efficiently clean and remove pathogens at your workplace or ours.

The MDU uses environmentally friendly, biodegradable detergents manufactured in New Zealand.

Still Cleaning These By Hand?

We can wash and disinfect with ultraviolet light (UV) in just 6 minutes removing dangerous pathogens from:

  • Wheelchairs
  • Commodes
  • Toilet Frames
  • Shower Seats
  • Overbed Tables
  • Crutches
  • Strollers
  • Physiotherapy Equipment
  • and more...

How does Our MDU work?

The MDU is an automated cleaning disinfection machine (comparable to a dishwasher for medical and disability equipment).

First up is a pressure wash and rinse from 2 powerful spray arms. The cleaning pressure from the sprays is 40-50 PSI, ensuring effective cleaning.

Next the Ultraviolet Sterilisation cycle (the same system hospitals use) ensures superior disinfection.
